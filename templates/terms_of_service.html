2. Google Account Data and Privacy

DegreeViz allows users to sign in using their Google accounts. If you choose to log in via Google, we request access to your basic profile information (specifically, your email address).

  • What we collect: We solely collect the email address associated with your log-in to create a unique identifier for saving your degree plans.
  • How we use it: Your email is used exclusively for authentication purposes and to link your saved course graphs to your specific account.
  • Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your email address or personal data with any third parties.

3. Data Storage

DegreeViz stores the course graphs you create (including course codes, credits, and layout positions) in our secure database so you can access them later. You can delete your saved graphs at any time from your dashboard, which permanently removes them from our servers.
